Common Questions

Got Questions? We Have Answers.

Everything you need to know about our Howard County junk removal and hauling services.

How do you price your services?

Our pricing is primarily based on the volume of space your items take up in our truck. For specialized jobs like moving or heavy hauling, we offer flat-rate quotes so you know exactly what to expect. No hidden fees, ever.

Are you insured?

Yes, 410 Odd Jobs LLC is fully insured. We take the safety of your property and our team very seriously, giving you peace of mind from start to finish.

Do you take hazardous materials?

We cannot take hazardous materials such as paints, chemicals, tires, or lithium batteries. If you're unsure about a specific item, just give us a call and we'll let you know!

Do you offer same-day service?

We often do! Depending on our schedule in Howard County, we can frequently accommodate same-day or next-day requests. Call (410) 800-6074 for immediate availability.

Where does the junk go?

We prioritize responsible disposal. Items in good condition are often donated to local charities, and we recycle as much as possible before heading to the landfill.
